BRIGHTWHEEL APP

A Redesign



Brightwheel is a parent-teacher communication app for pre-schools. It helps Teachers/Admins perform school work & send updates of the student's day to their parents.

THE OVERVIEW

Brightwheel is a parent-teacher communication app for pre-schools. The leadership team of Brightwheel app wanted to create a more user-friendly experience for Teachers & Administrators who visit this app for the first time.

Team: Worked as a part of a team of 3 UX Designers

Duration: 14 Days

Tools: Usability Testing, User Interviews, Sketching, Wireframes (Sketch 3), Prototyping (Invision)

THE PROBLEM

"My first time using brightwheel, its kind of confusing"

The first time users seem to have a difficult time navigating the flow of the brightwheel app

  • The interface has elements that look like a design logo vs a action button
  • Demo is provided deeper in the app
  • Navigation is not smooth

SOLUTION

Solution is creating a improved design to increase efficiency & ease-of-use to complete core tasks along with Reducing the number of steps to set up account

USABILITY TESTING

Test . Design . Repeat

We created paper prototypes, conducted usability testing of the current app & the new prototypes, redesigned based on the feedback received along with doing competetive & comparative analysis

INSIGHTS

  • Teacher's wanted a shorter Setup Wizard
  • Allergies & showing them on the app under the student's profile was of importance
  • Having Demo show up earlier was helpful
  • Brightwheel the client was aiming to create a bottom MENU & were struggling to make it work

FINAL DESIGN

Based upon usability testing & feedback from brightwheel
