MIND MAGIC

A Cognitive Gaming App.



A digital solution to allow family caregivers the ability to connect with their aging relatives & challenge each other to games that improve mental sharpness over time.

THE OVERVIEW

The leadership team of Bay alarm medical systems wants to reduce the reluctance among its subscribers to use their service by extending the utility of its service. This extension of the utility could be in the form an app or a website.

Team: Worked as a part of a team of 3 UX Designers

Duration: 14 Days

Tools: User Research, Personas, Scenarios, Wireframes, Prototype, Sketch 3, Invision

THE PROBLEM

"My daughter got this device I'm not sure I need it"

The seniors are reluctant to use the service/device because:

  • They are not familiar with the device or company
  • Trying to subconciously express Independence

RESEARCH

We created a survey, conducted in-person interviews, looked at secondary research & did competetive & comparative analysis

INSIGHTS

  • Family caregivers are concerned about their aging family member's mental health
  • Family caregivers would like to get a view of behavioral patterns to be able to detect changes
  • Seniors are uncomfortable with direct questions about mental & physical health
  • Using & doing familiar things is what seniors enjoy most

SOLUTION

The solution created is a cognitive gaming app which brings the family care giver & the elderly loved one together on a singel platform making the whole experience fun. The family care giver is able to connect to the senior & observe the behavioural patterns while the senior enjoys playing familiar games to help with mental sharpness & acumen
